Unicorn pHAT


  • 32 RGB LEDs (SK6218 - 3.5mm x 3.5mm)

  • LED data driven via DMA over PWM

  • Compatible with Raspberry Pi 3, 2, B+, A+, Zero, and Zero W

  • Female header requires soldering

  • Unicorn pHAT pinout
